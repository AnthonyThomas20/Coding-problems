## Addition of two numbers a Twist ##

Using a method, pass two variables and find the sum of two numbers.

**Test case 1:**

*Sample Input*

> Number 1 – 20

> Number 2 – 20.38

*Sample Output*

> Sum = 40.38

**Test case 2:**

*Sample Input*

> Number 1 – 35

> Number 2 – 47.89

*Sample Output*

> Sum = 82.89
