## FULLY AUTOMATIC VENDING MACHINE ## 

It dispenses your order on just press of button. A vending machine can serve range of products as follows:

**Coffee**

1. Espresso Coffee
2. Cappuccino Coffee
3. Latte Coffee

**Tea**

1. Plain Tea
2. Assam Tea
3. Ginger Tea
4. Cardamom Tea
5. Masala Tea
6. Lemon Tea
7. Green Tea
8. Organic Darjeeling Tea

**Soups**

1. Hot and Sour Soup
2. Veg Corn Soup
3. Tomato Soup
4. Spicy Tomato Soup

**Beverages**

1. Hot Chocolate
2. Badam Drink
3. Badam-Pista Drink

Write a program to take input for main menu & sub menu and display the name of sub manu selected in following format(Enter the first letter to select main menu):

Welcome to CCD
Enjoy your < name of sub menu >


**Test Case 1**

*Sample Input:*

> c

> 1


*Sample Output:*

> Welcome to CCD!

> Enjoy your Expresso Coffee!



**Test Case 2**

*Sample Input:*

> t

> 9

*Sample Output:*

> INVALID OPTION!
